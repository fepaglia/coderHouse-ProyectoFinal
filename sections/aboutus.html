- About Us -

If your desire is for your palate to enjoy an exquisite mix of flavors, textures and aromas, characteristic of Argentine cuisine, you can make it a reality at the hands of Chefs Federico and Micaela, who together with their great staff are part of Fedela's, one of the first class restaurants that the porte;o neighborhood of San Telmo has.

Federico:

"Fede" is the creator of Fedela's. He does not respond to the profile of a classic barbecue, he can be defined as a true chef: he selects the merchandise, tastes each dish and investigates trends. He is in each of the details, he is an all-terrain entrepreneur.

Micaela:

"Mica" is the alma mater of Fedela's. She started as a pastry assistant 4 years ago and little by little she earned the place through sacrifice and commitment. It was a lot of hard work. Until one day together with "Fede" decided to start this dream.
